Lasagna Recipe

Click me Description

This recipe gives the instructions to make a lasagna. Lasagna is an Italian-American dish that includes meat,cheese, pasta, and sauce. There are many different variations on this meal.

Different meats can be used to modify your options such as ground chicken, ground turkey, or a leaner beef mix. Its possible to use a ground pork as well. Different iterations may include homemade pasta, homemade cheese, or various homemade components.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Crushed tomatoes (2 cans)
  • Tomato paste (2 cans)
  • White sugar (2 teaspoons)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Fennel seeds
  • Italian seasoning
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest and serve
